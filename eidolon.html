Overview

AI Programmer | Creative Director
Fall 2024 - Present (Unreal Engine 5; Team of 7)

"Eidolon: Fate of Fools" is a classical arena shooter with a card game twist in which astral combatants compete using mystical abilities inspired by major arcana tarot cards.

As AI Programmer, I implement behaviors with emergent characteristics to allow AI combatants to appear player-like in a movement-oriented combat game.

As Design Lead, I document design goals, facilitate playtests, and iterate game features to reach desired project outcomes.

Responsibilities

  • Maintain design documentation to communicate deliverables and target outcomes to the team throughout production.
  • Develop player-like AI combatants to enable all groups of players to enjoy the experience of a full game lobby.
  • Oversee development and integration of game features to ensure design cohesion throughout the project.

Technical Highlights

  • Utilized a variety of navigation assets in each arena to enable AI bots to move fluidly and use special arena features.
  • Created custom air control behavior for AI bots to allow continuous reaction to the game state even when jumping or being launched.
  • Developed a targeting system incorporating AI sense features to allow bots to rapidly adapt during multiplayer combat.

Design Highlights

  • Introduced the mystical theming and tarot card influence to bolster card mechanics with evocative context.
  • Studied tarot games to incorporate traditional symbolism into novel game mechanics and card abilities.
  • Documented playtest procedures and facilitated multiplayer playtests to determine if design outcomes are effectively reached.
  • Wrote and implemented randomized usernames for bots, among other text assets, to make the game feel more alive.
